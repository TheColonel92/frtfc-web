Presentation of the Squadron

=FrTFC= French Tactical Forces is a squadron dedicated to strategy and teamwork in War Thunder. Join us for intense battles and shared victories!

Welcome to the FrTFC, we are a French-speaking squadron active in all combat modes. We keep all players with a high Activity rate who want to integrate and fight alongside us, from beginners wanting to learn, to experts to guide us. Especially during squadron battles!

Installation Steps

To install the sound mods, follow these steps:

1. Create the mod folder

Go to the War Thunder installation folder and create a new folder named mod in the sound/ directory (if not already done).

2. Download the mods

Download the compatible sound mod files for War Thunder from a reliable source and place the .bank in the sound/mod/ folder.
